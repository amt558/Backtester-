Research — LITE applied to Flow A (Pine → TradingView → Alpaca)

The §1 confound from CALIBRATED dissolves under TV-only architecture. There is no Python equivalent to drift from Pine — Pine is the source of truth for both backtest and execution. Backtest baseline = pine_archive/<card_id>/tv_trades.csv (frozen at Accept). Live trades arrive via TV alert webhook → receiver (port 8878) → Alpaca, tagged with client_order_id for native attribution (Slice -0.5, shipped). Tracking error is a distributional comparison (live PnL distribution vs frozen TV-backtest distribution), not a per-bar fill match.
